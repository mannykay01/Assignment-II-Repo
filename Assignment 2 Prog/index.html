Lionel Messi

Argentine footballer

BIOGRAPHY

Lionel Andrés "Leo" Messi (born on June 24, 1987) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. In 2012, he set a record for most goals in a calendar year and, a decade later, helped the Argentina national team win its third FIFA World Cup. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, and was named the world's best player for a record eight times by FIFA. The eight-time Ballon d’Or winner moved to Paris Saint-Germain in 2021, and in July 2023, he joined Inter Miami.
